# DevOps and infrastructure Trends - Week 48, 2025

## Main Aspects

- Importance of understanding outages and their impact on services
- Centralization of DevOps tools and platforms
- Integration of CI/CD processes with various tools

## Why It Became Important

Understanding the causes and impacts of outages is crucial for improving system resilience and developing better architectural practices. Additionally, as organizations adopt CI/CD and DevOps practices, the centralization and optimization of tools become essential for maintaining efficiency and performance in software delivery.

## Tools & Frameworks

- GitLab
- Jira
- Kubernetes
- Docker
- Cloudflare

## Suggested Actions for Engineers

- Stay informed about recent outages and analyze their causes to improve your own systems.
- Focus on optimizing and centralizing your DevOps tools to enhance collaboration and efficiency.
- Integrate CI/CD pipelines with other essential tools to streamline development processes and ensure robust security practices.
