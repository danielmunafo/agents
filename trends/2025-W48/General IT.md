# General IT Trends - Week 48, 2025

## Main Aspects

- Integration of AI in business processes
- The importance of innovation culture
- Emergence of new roles and skills in technology

## Why It Became Important

These trends are important because they signify a shift in how businesses operate, with AI becoming an essential part of operational efficiency. Additionally, fostering an innovation-friendly culture is crucial for companies to adapt and thrive in a rapidly changing technological landscape.

## Tools & Frameworks

- SAP S/4HANA
- AWS
- SUSE guide for digital transformation
- Innovation Maturity Model

## Suggested Actions for Engineers

- Embrace AI technologies and integrate them into workflows for improved efficiency.
- Cultivate a culture that encourages experimentation and accepts failure as part of the innovation process.
- Stay updated on emerging roles and skills, and invest in continuous learning and development.
